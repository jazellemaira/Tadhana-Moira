Moira Dela Torre

About

Hello, I'm Moira Dela Torre, and I welcome you into the melodic realm of my life. Born on November 4, 1993, in Olongapo City, Philippines, my journey as a singer, songwriter, and music producer has been nothing short of a heartfelt adventure. From a young age, music became my refuge, a language that allowed me to express the depth of my emotions. My introduction to the broader audience came through the second season of "The Voice of the Philippines" in 2014, where I poured my soul into each performance, earning a place in the finals and a platform to share my passion with the world.

Since then, my musical exploration has led me to create songs that resonate with the human experience. Tracks like "Malaya," "Titibo-Tibo," and "Tagpuan" have become anthems for love, heartbreak, and self-discovery, reflecting the intricacies of life through my unique lens. Collaboration has been a beautiful aspect of my journey, allowing me to stretch my artistic boundaries across genres and styles. From mainstream pop to inspirational and worship songs, each collaboration has been a learning experience, adding layers to my musical identity.

Beyond the spotlight, I've delved into philanthropy and advocacy, driven by a desire to use my influence for positive change. My authenticity and relatability have forged connections with fans, turning them into a supportive community that fuels my creative spirit. As I continue to evolve as an artist, my music remains a testament to the shared human experience. With each note and every lyric, I invite you to join me on this ongoing odyssey of self-expression, emotion, and connection. Together, let's explore the tapestry of life through the universal language of music.

Moira Dela Torre
